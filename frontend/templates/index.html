Formula 1 Data Explorer

Welcome to our database systems final project! We are exploring historical Formula 1 data to uncover insights about race strategies, driver performance, and more.

Available Analyses

  • Pit Stop Strategy: Explore how the number of pit stops correlates with a driver's final finishing position.
  • Drivers Analytics: Analyze driver experience, win percentages, nationalities, and overtaking ability.
  • Circuits Analytics: Discover the most dangerous tracks, historical host countries, and track-specific specialists.
  • Seasons & Constructors: Look into historical dominance by teams and the most unpredictable seasons in F1 history.
